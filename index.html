Licensing and Certification

The first thing to look for in a garbage collection service is whether the company is fully licensed and certified to operate in your area. Certification shows that the company complies with local regulations and adheres to industry standards. It's also important to ask if the employees are trained to handle different types of waste safely, especially hazardous materials. Licensed companies are more likely to provide reliable and professional services compared to unregulated providers.
